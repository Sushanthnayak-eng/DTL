About Compost Quality Analyzer

The Compost Quality Analyzer is a machine learning-powered web application designed to evaluate the quality of compost based on scientific parameters. It predicts whether the compost is suitable for agricultural use and provides actionable suggestions to improve poor-quality compost.

Features:

  • Machine learning-based compost classification
  • Automated improvement suggestions
  • User-friendly interface
  • Secure login access
  • Real-time predictions

Developed For:

Students, farmers, waste management teams, researchers, and environmental projects focused on sustainable agriculture and compost quality improvement.
